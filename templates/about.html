About TruthLens

Bringing transparency to the information age.

Our Mission

In an era of rapid information spread, distinguishing fact from fiction is more challenging than ever. TruthLens leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze text patterns and identify potential misinformation.

How It Works

We use a Logistic Regression model trained on the LIAR dataset, a benchmark for fake news detection. Our system analyzes:

  • Vocabulary and phrasing
  • Sentiment analysis
  • Linguistic patterns specific to reputable vs. unreliable sources

The Technology

Built with Python, Flask, and Scikit-Learn. The model uses TF-IDF vectorization to convert text into meaningful numerical features for classification.
